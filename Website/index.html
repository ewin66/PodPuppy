Welcome

Podpuppy is a free Podcatcher for Windows. You can use it to subscribe to Podcasts and automatically receive sound and video files as they are released by the author. PodPuppy is very easy to use while supporting all the major podcast formats and providing all the essential functions of a podcast receiver.

I made PodPuppy in my spare time and I will continue to support it and add features as they are requested. PodPuppy will always be free.

Checkout the news section below for the latest announcements. You can download the latest version of PodPuppy from the downloads page. If you want to discuss PodPuppy with fellow users, head over ot the forum .

~Felix
